A little about myself

Hello there! My name is Michael E. Barreiro, and I am a Software / Web Developer from New Orleans, Louisiana. I have always had a passion for art and the creative aspects of things. Building and creating things from scratch especially intrigue me.

As you can see, I am a sucker for front-end work, and I can’t stay away from CSS3 an HTML5! Although I definitely prefer front-end work, I realize that it’s not always about front-end, and I have spent a large portion of my time at the NashvilleSoftware Institute (NSS) perfecting my back-end work as well. I am a dedicated hard-worker, determined to succeed no matter what!

Through the Nashville Software School, I have learned a large range of languages: from angular.js and node.js to mobile video games. I have even been able to upload apps that I made in class onto the Google Play store, and learned how to deploy to the Itune store as well.
