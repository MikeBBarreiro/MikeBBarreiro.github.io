My Story

I am a dork with a dream bigger then me, but dorks with big dreams have to start small, just like everyone else. My name is Michael Barreiro, and here is my story. I was born and raised in New Orleans, Louisiana.I always had a passion for tech and what it was capable of. Then suddenly, code jumped into my life and I was instantly in love with it. Not only did it jumped into my life, but it completly changed it. I worked extra hours at a grocery store to save up money, and when the time was right, I dropped everything and moved to Nashville to go to a school called Nashville Software School (NSS). Yes the odds were against me; I was attedning an intense bootcamp specialized in teaching software & web development that kept me up till 3 with homework. I had limited money with few friends in a new place I had never been to, but I told my self in time, "this would all be worth it!". And guess what my friend.. It was.. I graduated, got my dream job, and I wont stop there. I feind for more knowledge, I want to be the best or even better then what I can be. When I looked for a job, I looked past the pay and asked myself, "What knowledge can I get here from the experience? What new languages will I need to learn"? I work for the knowledge, not for the paycheck. The paycheck just comes with the knowledge. I want to grow as a developer, and theres nothing out there that is going to stop me!
